Items of the Day

Rimpang : Efek Rumah Kaca's return to the Indonesian indie scene

'Rimpang' was ERK's attempt to return to a simple alternative pop formula. According to Cholil et al, this album tries to convince us that even the smallest political efforts have an impact. How does their message fare during our current election cycle?

Gabber Selera Nusantara : Suling and 808s

Gabber Modus Operandi shows us the future of Indonesia's experimental electronic music : through the amalgamation of blaring traditional flutes and thumping gabber.
